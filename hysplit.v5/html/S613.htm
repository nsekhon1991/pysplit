Advanced / Configuration Setup / Trajectory Points Output Frequency

The endpoint write interval TOUT(60) sets the time interval in minutes at which trajectory end-point positions will be written to the output file. Output intervals of less than 60 minutes can be selected. This will also force the internal time step to be an even multiple of the output interval.

The maximum trajectory duration KHMAX (9999) is the maximum duration of any one trajectory in hours.
